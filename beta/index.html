Join the Kidence family beta.

Kidence pairs a kid‑friendly wearable with an early‑detection model that notices the physiological signs of emotional overwhelm before things boil over — tantrums, meltdowns, frustration, anxiety, and tough transitions.

When an early signal appears, Kidence coaches you on what to say and do in the moment, and helps your child learn to recognize and understand their own feelings — so hard moments get caught earlier, pass faster, and moods become more stable over time.

We are inviting a small group of families into our early beta. The beta may include a lightweight wearable, short parent check‑ins, and optional notes about emotional outbursts or difficult routines.

This is not a medical device, diagnosis tool, or therapy service. We are learning from families and testing whether real‑time signals and parent coaching can help reduce the frequency, intensity, or duration of difficult emotional moments.
